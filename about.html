Our Mission & Vision

At Stridex, our mission is to empower individuals with limb differences by providing advanced, affordable, and intelligent prosthetic solutions.

We envision a world where mobility challenges are overcome with smart technology, enabling everyone to walk forward with confidence and dignity.

Our Story

Stridex was born out of a shared passion among a group of engineers and designers who saw the challenges faced by amputees. What started as a college innovation project quickly turned into a mission to build next-generation prosthetics.

We witnessed how traditional solutions lacked affordability and intelligence. That's why we decided to create a product that not only restores mobility but also adapts to the user’s lifestyle with AI and smart design. Today, we proudly deliver hope and technology through every step.
